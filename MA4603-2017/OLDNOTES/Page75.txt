

another use of the scatter plot is the detection of outliers. An outlier is a data point that doesnt fit the pattern of the rest of the data.

There can be several reasons for an outlier including mistakes made in data entyr, or simply an unusual value.

Each Outlier must be investigated. The usual approach is to analyse the data including the suspect point, the redo the analysis without the point, and examine the difference in results.
